# Chat With Your Documents

## The Problem

You have PDFs, manuals, SOPs, or reports that nobody reads when they need to. Your team wastes time digging through folders or asking the same questions over and over. Generic AI tools don't know your stuff — and you don't want to paste sensitive business data into random web tools.

## What You Get

A working chat interface connected to your documents. Upload your PDFs, DOCX files, or text files — then ask questions in plain language and get instant answers with sources.

It runs on your own server or a private cloud. Your data never leaves your control.

## How It Works

1. **Send me your docs** — Drop your files in a folder or share a drive link
2. **I build the bot** — Ingests your docs, sets up the search, deploys a clean chat UI
3. **You use it** — Chat with your documents, add new ones anytime
