## Usage Notes

- **Warm outreach first**: Start with people who know you. Higher response rate.
- **Personalize the bracketed parts**: Generic = ignored.
- **Send 5-10/week**: Quality over quantity.
- **Follow up once**: Most responses come from follow-ups.
- **Track in a spreadsheet**: Who you contacted, when, response.
